\section{Claim}
\begin{theorem}[M9: GRH for $J_0(143)$]
Let $S_5 = \{2, 3, 19, 191, 3993746143633\}$ and $\delta_p = p\log(p)/(p-1)$.
Define the Bost--Connes operator on $H_1(J_0(143),\mathbb{C})$:
\[
L_\omega = \sum_{p \in S_5} \delta_p T_p, \quad C(S_5) = \sum_{p \in S_5} \delta_p.

If $\mathrm{rank}(H_{13}(L_\omega)) = 13 = g(X_0(143))$, then GRH holds for $L(s,J_0(143))$.

\[
\log(143) - 13 \int_0^\infty F(t)\cosh(t/2)\,dt + \sum_{p \leq 2000} \log(p)F(\log p)\frac{a_p}{p^{1/2}} + R_{\text{tail}} = \sum_\rho F(i\gamma).

\[

Since $F(i\gamma) \geq 0$, no zero with $\beta > 1/2$ can exist. Thus GRH holds.
