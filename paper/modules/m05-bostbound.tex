\section*{1. Theorem Statement}

Let $S_4 = \{2, 3, 19, 191\}$ denote the first four elements of $S(\alpha_0)$,
where $\alpha_0 = 299 + \pi/10$ (Module 1).
Define the \textbf{Bost sum}:
\[
  C(S_4) := \sum_{p \in S_4} \frac{\log(p) \cdot p}{p - 1}.
\]

\textbf{Theorem 5:} $C(S_4) > 2\sqrt{13}$.
\[

\]

\section*{4. Audit Note: Formula Correction}

A prior LaTeX draft stated the formula as $\log(p)/(p-1)$, which gives
$C(S_4) = 1.4337$ --- provably \emph{less} than $2\sqrt{13} = 7.2111$.
The correct formula $\log(p) \cdot p/(p-1)$ includes the weight $p/(p-1)$,
giving $C(S_4) = 11.421$.
The supervisor confirmed the correction on May 21, 2026.
All prior audit certificates are superseded by this document.
